IEEE SPACE 2026

A Unified Habitat-Integrated Autonomous Rover Framework for Structured Lunar Surface Operations

✅ Accepted for Presentation

A modular ROS2-based autonomous rover framework designed to support astronauts inside structured lunar habitats through intelligent navigation, environmental monitoring and autonomous decision making.

Artificial Intelligence ROS2 SLAM LiDAR Navigation Lunar Robotics

Behind the Research

This research began with my curiosity about autonomous systems for future space exploration. While working on VyomBot, I started wondering how intelligent robotic assistants could support astronauts during long-duration lunar missions. That curiosity gradually evolved into a structured research problem and eventually became my first accepted IEEE research.
